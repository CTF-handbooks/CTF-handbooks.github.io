This GitHub pages website is a collection of handbooks for basic guidelines on a variety of tasks such as making maps and testing them for the Minetest Capture the Flag game by rubenwardy, written primarily with the main server in mind. The authors of this website and the community members gladly will welcome your efforts to bring the realm of CTF to new heights. Even the smallest of steps, when summed, can add up to great leaps!
